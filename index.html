About Me

I hold a Ph.D. from the Department of Management Sciences at the University of Pune (PUMBA). Currently serving as the Coordinator at the Modern Institute of Business Management in Pune since 2010, my dedication to education and leadership extends beyond academia. As the Pradesh Upadhyaksha of Bharatiya Janata Yuva Morcha in Maharashtra, I actively contribute to political engagement. I have also served as the Ex-President of the Yuvati Cell at Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and held the position of Deputy Secretary at the Progressive Education Society in Pune. My diverse experiences in academia and public service reflect my commitment to both educational excellence and community leadership.

Pune District - Northern Constituency (Shirur Lok Sabha)

In the city of Shirur, Pune district, a meeting of the district-level office bearers and activists of the Bharatiya Janata Yuva Morcha (BJYM) was held at the office of Niteen Pacharne, the President of the BJP in Shirur. Sandip Aaba Satav, the district president of BJYM, chaired the gathering, which included Mandal officials and workers from the Shirur Mandal. Ganesh Bapu Kute, the Pradesh Upadhyaksha of BJYM, and Gaurav Zhurunge, the district secretary, along with Umesha Shelke, the BJYM President in Shirur city, and several other office bearers, were present at the meeting. The discussions covered various upcoming events and initiatives of the party, including the Namo Chashak, and detailed deliberations took place on the youth organization's activities. During the event, a significant number of youth actively participated in the discussions, showcasing their enthusiasm and commitment to the BJYM.

Women's Safety Meet

In the context of the recent heinous attack on a young woman in the vicinity of Perugate Chowk in Sadashiv Peth, representatives of the BJP Yuva Morcha (Pune City) met with DCP Gill Saheb to discuss concrete measures regarding women's safety and to request strict punishment for the accused. Gauri Dudhal, Vice President of Yuva Aghadi, Sarika Warule, and social activists from Sadashiv Peth were present during this discussion. They urged for stringent action against the perpetrator and emphasized the need for enhanced women's safety measures.
